MICROHABITS

A NEW BOOK FROM GROVE AVE

Achieving Personal Growth Through Small, Mindful Changes

COMING OCTOBER 2016

Want to live a productive and fulfilling life? Trying a few tactics but not happy with where you are?

Making significant changes to our lives is challenging. Undoing our ingrained habits all at once can be daunting. And even if you manage to make those big changes, what good are they if you revert back to your old ways after just a few weeks?

We feel your pain. We’ve been frustrated by what’s out there: the “get results quick” tactics that are often too good to be true and the very involved “systems” that force us to abandon our way of life in a manner that is often unsustainable and too difficult to adopt all at once. We decided instead to write a book based on a more tangible and gradual approach. Our approach is based on the concept of microhabits--small, repeatable behaviors that, when taken together, can add up to big, impactful changes.

The book is titled Microhabits: Achieving Personal Growth Through Small, Mindful Changes and is scheduled to come out sometime in October 2016. It’ll feature dozens of actionable microhabits as well as the introduction to our Foundation of Growth framework. We’re incredibly excited and know you’ll find it useful.
