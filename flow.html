Ingredients

Grove Ave Flow combines two compounds that assist in getting your mind into a productive groove.

NOOPEPT

Noopept is a protein-like compound that is commonly used to alleviate symptoms of Alzheimer’s disease due to its range of neuroprotective effects. Studies have shown that it prevents inflammation, facilitates the removal of unwanted toxins in the brain, and also positively impacts general cognitive performance. Noopept increases levels of a brain-signalling molecule, called acetylcholine. Through its action on this signalling pathway, noopept provides an array of cognitive benefits, boosting long and short term memory performance, attentional ability, and mood.

CHOLINE BITARTRATE

Choline bitartrate is a compound found in many common foods such as eggs, spinach, and peanuts, and is considered an important nutrient used by our body to make essential molecules that are necessary for health. Choline is a building block for the signalling molecule acetylcholine. By boosting acetylcholine production, choline bitartrate has been shown to increase the rate of cognitive recovery in people with traumatic brain injury, and is a general cognitive enhancer, improving memory and performance on word recognition tasks.
