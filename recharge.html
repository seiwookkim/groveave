Ingredients

Grove Ave Recharge combines two plant extracts that help you unwind and relieve stress.

RHODIOLA ROSEA

Rhodiola rosea is a mountainous flowering plant that grows all over the world and has been used for centuries in Asian and European herbal medicine to reduce stress and prevent altitude sickness. Salidroside, a sugar-type molecule found in rhodiola rosea, acts on the stress pathway by reducing the amount of stress hormones and other chemical signals of stress in your body. The plant extract has been the subject of many scientific studies that show its effectiveness in alleviating stress and improving physical performance.

BACOPA MONNIERI

Bacopa monnieri is a medicinal herb which has been used for thousands of years in India for its beneficial effects on learning and memory. The active chemicals in bacopa are called bacosides, which increase the levels of different neurotransmitters in the brain to improve communication between different brain areas. Many clinical studies of humans treated with bacopa have shown significant increases in memory, as well as stress reduction.
